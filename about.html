Leon Leng is a Ph.D. researcher working on distributed intelligence systems, programmable governance, and the intersection of AI agents, blockchain economics, and energy infrastructure.

He focuses on how intelligent systems allocate resources, align incentives, and balance centralization with autonomy. Leon collaborates with academic institutions and frontier companies to design operationally intelligent systems that are efficient, resilient, and interpretable.
